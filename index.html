Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our AI-powered job scam detection system and how it helps protect job seekers.

How accurate is your AI detection system?

Our system achieves 95% confidence in identifying legitimate job postings and an 85% detection rate for fraudulent listings. This high performance is achieved through our advanced SBERT embeddings and HistGradientBoosting classifier with SMOTE balancing, ensuring reliable protection for job seekers.

What technology powers your detection system?

We use Sentence-BERT (SBERT) for advanced text understanding, converting job descriptions into semantic vectors. Our machine learning pipeline includes smart text processing, HistGradientBoosting classification, and SMOTE balancing. The system is deployed using Flask and Docker for reliable, real-time analysis.

What aspects of job postings do you analyze?

Our system performs comprehensive analysis of multiple aspects including job titles, company profiles, job descriptions, requirements, benefits, and more. The SBERT model creates semantic vectors that capture subtle patterns and relationships in the text, helping identify suspicious or fraudulent content.

How quickly can I get results?

Our system provides instant, real-time analysis through our containerized Flask API. When you submit a job posting, our AI processes it immediately, analyzing thousands of data points to provide you with accurate fraud detection results within seconds. The Docker deployment ensures consistent and reliable performance.
